What is Collateral Token

Changpeng Zhao (CZ), founder of Binance, has consistently warned crypto businesses with these golden rules:

CZ's Golden Rules:

Never use a token as collateral
Don't borrow if you run a crypto business
Don't use capital "efficiently"
Have a large reserve

So we broke all these rules and legally created a company using our token as collateral to buy more BNB.

How It Works

We have legally registered a company specifically for our memecoin. Using our company registration and token valuation, we are leveraging our cryptocurrency holdings as collateral.

This collateral allows us to secure loans to purchase more BNB and increase the supply of our own coin. By utilizing our token's market value, we're creating a cyclical growth mechanism that expands our holdings across multiple assets.

The company structure provides legal legitimacy while the token valuation gives us the financial leverage needed to execute this strategy at scale.
