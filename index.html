What is Pump Or Treat?

Pump Or Treat is an innovative token distribution mechanism built on pump.fun that automatically rewards holders every 120 seconds with their proportional share of trading fees.

How It Works

  • Automatic Rewards: Every 120 seconds, accumulated trading fees are distributed to all token holders
  • Proportional Distribution: Your airdrop amount is based on your percentage of total token supply
  • No Claiming Required: Rewards appear directly in your wallet automatically
  • Fair Launch: Starting market cap of just $5,000 ensures early holders get maximum benefits

Token Economics

  • Starting Market Cap: $5,000
  • Distribution Interval: 120 seconds
  • Fee Structure: Trading fees accumulate and are distributed proportionally
  • Initial Airdrops: Starting at ~0.01 SOL, scaling with market cap growth

Getting Started

  • Purchase Pump Or Treat tokens on pump.fun
  • Hold tokens in your wallet
  • Receive automatic airdrops every 120 seconds
  • Watch your rewards grow as trading volume increases

FAQ

Q: Do I need to claim my rewards?

A: No, all airdrops are sent automatically to your wallet every 120 seconds.

Q: How is my airdrop amount calculated?

A: Your airdrop is proportional to your token holdings. If you hold 1% of the total supply, you receive 1% of the airdrop pool.

Q: When do airdrops start?

A: Airdrops begin immediately after launch and continue every 120 seconds as long as there is trading activity.
