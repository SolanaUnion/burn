Documentation

Welcome to Build and Burn

Build and Burn is a decentralized token burning platform that allows users to permanently remove unwanted tokens from their cryptocurrency wallets. This documentation will guide you through the process of using our platform safely and effectively.

What is Token Burning?

Token burning is the process of permanently removing cryptocurrency tokens from circulation by sending them to an unrecoverable wallet address. Once tokens are burned, they cannot be retrieved or used again.

Why Burn Tokens?

  • Clean Up Your Wallet: Remove spam tokens or unwanted airdrops
  • Privacy: Eliminate tokens that may compromise your privacy
  • Organization: Keep your wallet organized with only the tokens you want
  • Deflationary Effect: Reduce token supply for tokens you hold

How to Use Build and Burn

Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner. We support the following wallets:

  • MetaMask
  • WalletConnect
  • Coinbase Wallet
  • Trust Wallet

Step 2: Select Tokens to Burn

Once connected, you'll see a list of all tokens in your wallet. Select the tokens you wish to burn by checking the boxes next to them. You can also specify the amount of each token to burn.

Step 3: Review and Confirm

Review your selection carefully. The burning process is irreversible. Once you confirm, the tokens will be permanently destroyed.

Step 4: Sign the Transaction

Approve the transaction in your wallet. You'll need to pay a small gas fee to complete the burning process. The exact fee depends on network congestion.

⚠️ Important Warning Token burning is permanent and irreversible. Once tokens are burned, they cannot be recovered. Always double-check your selection before confirming the transaction. We recommend burning small test amounts first if you're unsure.

Gas Fees

Burning tokens requires paying network gas fees. The exact amount varies based on:

  • Current network congestion
  • Number of tokens being burned
  • Blockchain network you're using

We recommend checking current gas prices before initiating a burn. You can use tools like etherscan.io to monitor gas fees.

Security Best Practices

Verify Contract Addresses

Always verify that you're interacting with the legitimate Build and Burn smart contract. Our official contract addresses are published on our website and verified on blockchain explorers.

Use Hardware Wallets

For maximum security, we recommend using a hardware wallet (Ledger, Trezor) when burning significant amounts of tokens.

Test First

If you're burning tokens for the first time, consider burning a small test amount first to familiarize yourself with the process.

FAQ

Can I recover burned tokens?

No. Token burning is permanent and irreversible. Once tokens are sent to the burn address, they cannot be retrieved.

Do I receive anything for burning tokens?

Build and Burn does not provide compensation for burned tokens. The service exists solely to help you remove unwanted tokens from your wallet.

Is there a minimum or maximum burn amount?

You can burn any amount of tokens from your wallet, from a fraction of a token to your entire balance. However, ensure the transaction value exceeds the gas fee cost.

What happens to burned tokens?

Burned tokens are sent to a verifiable burn address (typically 0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD) where they become permanently inaccessible and are removed from circulation.
