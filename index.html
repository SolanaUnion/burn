THE SOLANAU RESERVE

The First Digital Reserve Backed by Physical Gold

What We Do

The SOLANAU Reserve creates the world's first transparent digital gold reserve using fees from pump.fun transactions. Every fee collected goes directly into purchasing physical gold, building a real, tangible reserve that backs digital assets.

How It Works

Step 1

Fees are collected from pump.fun token transactions on the Solana blockchain.

Step 2

Fees are converted and allocated for physical gold purchases.

Step 3

Physical gold is purchased and stored in certified vaults.

Step 4

Every purchase is recorded on the blockchain for complete transparency.
