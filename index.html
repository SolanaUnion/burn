What is 4wheel?

4wheel is a revolutionary flywheel mechanism built on top of the four.meme platform's new developer incentive program. We leverage the 5% fee distribution to create sustainable, compounding returns for the community.

5%

Developer Fee Distribution: four.meme recently announced that developers now receive 5% of total platform fees once their coin reaches a market cap of 44,444.

How It Works

  • Deploy tokens on four.meme platform
  • Once market cap hits 44,444, developers receive 5% of all trading fees
  • 4wheel automatically reinvests these fees into the ecosystem
  • Creates a compounding flywheel effect for sustainable growth
  • Community benefits from continuous value creation

The Flywheel Effect

Our flywheel mechanism creates a self-reinforcing cycle of growth:

  • Launch Phase: Deploy tokens and build community momentum
  • Activation: Hit 44,444 market cap threshold to unlock 5% fees
  • Accumulation: Collect trading fees from platform activity
  • Reinvestment: Deploy fees back into ecosystem development
  • Growth: Increased activity generates more fees, spinning the wheel faster
